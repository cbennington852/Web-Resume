Gonzaga University, Daniel Olivares January 2024 - August 2024

I work one on one with the professor during the school year, we primarily work on research focusing on human learning and teaching.

Typically Computer Science Students are though of as solitary creatures. Students that do not interact with their peers. Oftentimes schoolwork is designed with this in mind, discouraging collaboration.

This outlook, however is rather discouraging, especially to new computer science students. Our reasearch is into building a tool that facilitates collaboration, and presents challenges to students in fun "gamified" ways, to get students engaged

Over the past year We build a VScode extension, that allows students to complete challenges, and communicate with classmates, Similar to Leetcode and Piazza, but rolled into the students coding editor

Technologies used
  • Typescript
  • JavaScript
  • HTML, CSS
  • NodeJs
  • MongoDB

This year I attended and presented at SIRC 2025(Spokane Inter-college Research Conference). The experience was a ton of fun, and I really enjoyed myself. Presenting gave me some insights into my own research, as well was walking about and reading other students papers.
