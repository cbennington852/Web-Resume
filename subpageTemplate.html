I work one on one with the professor during the school year, we primarily work on research focusing on human learning and teaching.

Students enrolled in introductory computer science courses tend towards individual work because of pedagogical practices discouraging collaboration and a focus on individual assignments. This can discourage new computer science students and may negatively affect persistence in computer science.

We are working to build tools that will foster growth and collaboration between computer science students.

We have published a paper on this topic. This project means a lot to me because it has the potential to positively impact how students collaborate and learn in computer science education. By fostering a more inclusive and cooperative learning environment, we hope to improve student engagement and retention in the field.
